FAQ

West Jordan questions.

Where is Vietopia Bistro West Jordan located?

1407 West 9000 South, West Jordan, UT 84088. We're on the 9000 South corridor, easy to find from Bangerter Highway or Redwood Road.

Is there parking?

Yes. Free parking in the lot in front of the restaurant.

Is the food the same as the Taylorsville location?

Yes. Same family, same recipes, same broth simmered overnight at each kitchen.

Do you take large groups?

Yes, with a heads-up. Call 801-255-5364 for parties over eight and we'll set up the dining room for you.

Do you cater out of West Jordan?

Yes. Either kitchen can handle catering across Salt Lake County. See the catering page for packages.
