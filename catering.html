Packages

Four ways to feed a group.

Pho Bar

Best for sit-down events

Twelve-hour broth in a warmer, fresh rice noodles, your choice of proteins, full herb-and-condiment bar. Guests build their own bowl.

Starts at 25 guests. Vegan and GF options included.

Banh Mi Platter

Best for office lunches

Pre-built banh mi cut into halves, mixed proteins (pork, chicken, vegetarian), pickles and herbs on the side. Holds well for an hour.

Starts at 10 guests. Easy to grab and go.

Bun Bowls

Best for outdoor or summer

Cold rice vermicelli bowls with grilled proteins, herbs, pickles, peanuts, and nuoc cham. No heat needed.

Starts at 15 guests. Light and refreshing.

Family Party Trays

Best for parties and holidays

Big trays of grilled pork, spring rolls, fried rice, com tam-style proteins, and dipping sauces. Self-serve.

Scales 25 to 100 plus.

Group sizes

From small office to big event.

  • 10 – 24 guests: Banh mi platters, bun bowls, or rice bowl trays. Pickup or delivery.
  • 25 – 49 guests: Pho bar option opens. Choose one or two protein platters plus sides.
  • 50 – 99 guests: Full pho bar, multiple proteins, dedicated set-up. Best with at least one week notice.
  • 100+ guests: Weddings, conferences, large company lunches. Custom menu, custom timeline.

FAQ

Catering questions.

What's the minimum group size for catering?

Ten people. From there we scale to one hundred plus. Smaller orders are easy to handle as regular takeout from either location.

How far in advance should I book?

Forty-eight hours minimum for groups under twenty-five. A week or more for larger events. We'll always tell you if a shorter timeline is workable.

Can you accommodate vegan and gluten-free guests?

Yes. We run a separate vegan broth, gluten-free rice noodles, and clearly labeled platters for dietary needs. Tell us the headcount split when you book.

Do you deliver and set up?

Yes across Salt Lake County and into Silicon Slopes. We deliver hot food in insulated carriers, set up the table, and pick everything up after. Pickup is also an option.

What's the most popular package for offices?

Banh mi platters and rice bowls. Easy to grab, no plate logistics, and they hold well for an hour. Pho bars are better for sit-down events where the broth can stay hot.
