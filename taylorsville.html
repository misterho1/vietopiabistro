FAQ

Taylorsville questions.

Where is Vietopia Bistro Taylorsville located?

5308 South Redwood Road, Taylorsville, UT 84123. We're on the Redwood Road corridor, near the Walmart and just south of 5400 South.

Is there parking?

Yes. Free parking in the shared lot directly in front of the restaurant.

Do you deliver from Taylorsville?

Pickup is available through our Order Online page. Delivery runs through the major third-party apps.

Can I bring a group?

Yes. The dining room comfortably seats parties up to twelve. For larger groups, give us a call ahead and we'll set up the back side.

Do you take reservations?

We're walk-in. For parties of eight or more, call 801-542-0780 and we'll save a table.
